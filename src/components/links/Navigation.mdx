## Usage

### Elevated Button

Elevated button will be used of necessary to provided seperation on background or floating button.

<Canvas of={Navigation.Elevated} />

### Filled Button

Filled Button is important to provide emphasis on call to action or final action.

<Canvas of={Navigation.Filled} />

### Filled Tonal Button

Filled tonal button should be use as alternative to filled button, their are useful in the lower priority.

<Canvas of={Navigation.FilledTonal} />

### Outlined Button

Outlined buttons are be used for non primary action in an app. Outlined buttons pair well with filled buttons to indicate an alternative, secondary action.

<Canvas of={Navigation.Outlined} />

### Text Button

Text buttons are used for the lowest priority actions, especially when presenting multiple options.

<Canvas of={Navigation.Text} />
